Ayam Pansuh

Ayam pansuh or manuk pansuh is a dish prepared by cooking chicken meat in a bamboo stalk, filled with water (which will later be the soup), seasonings and covered with tapioca leaves from the cassava plant

Umai

'Umai' is a famous traditional Melanau raw fish salad. Slices of fresh fish are garnished with thinly sliced onions, chillies and drizzled with vinegar, salt, and lime juice to make this humble yet mouthwatering delicacy

Kek Lapis Sarawak

Kek Lapis, also known as Sarawak Layer Cake, is a traditional layered cake from the Malaysian state of Sarawak. It is known for its vibrant colors and intricate patterns, often featuring multiple layers that are meticulously baked one at a time.

Kuih Bongkol

Kuih Bongkol is a traditional Malay delicacy, particularly popular in the state of Sarawak, Malaysia. It's a type of steamed dessert made from rice flour and coconut milk, often sweetened with palm sugar. The kuih is typically wrapped in banana leaves, giving it a distinct flavor and aroma from the leaves.
